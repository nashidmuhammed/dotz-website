Dotz: Track. Manage. Grow

A SaaS-based ERP to streamline inventory, sales, purchases, and financials for businesses of all sizes.

Core Features

User Management

Multi-user support with role-based access (Admin, Manager, etc.) for secure collaboration.

Inventory Management

Track products with multi-units, brands, categories, tax settings, and real-time stock levels.

Sales & Purchases

Create invoices, orders, returns, and estimates with seamless conversions and tax handling.

Financial Management

Manage accounts, receipts, payments, and generate profit & loss, balance sheets, and tax reports.

Dashboard & Analytics

Customizable dashboards with graphical insights for sales, inventory, and financials.

Security

Two-factor authentication, encrypted data, and audit logs* for robust protection.

Additional Features

Customizations

Configure negative stock, low stock alerts, currency formats, and custom invoice templates.

Tax & Compliance

Support for GST with GSTR-1, GSTR-3B reports for seamless compliance.

Multi-Platform Sharing

Share invoices via email, WhatsApp, or other platforms directly from Dotz.

Hosting Options

Cloud-hosted SaaS or custom domain hosting for a branded experience.

CRM Basics

Track customer interactions, leads, and follow-ups within sales modules*.

Supplier Management

Future Roadmap

AI Implementation

AI-powered summaries, predictive analytics, fraud detection, and a chat assistant for smarter decisions.

Automation Tools

Recurring invoices, payments, reminders, and advanced workflow automations for efficiency.

Expanded Modules

HRM for payroll and attendance, POS for retail, project management, and e-commerce integrations.

Advanced ERP Tools

Barcode/QR scanning, automated data import/export, and smart search across modules.

Multi-Currency Support

Business Intelligence

Customizable dashboards, KPI tracking, and advanced data visualizations.

Pricing Plans

Pro Plan
